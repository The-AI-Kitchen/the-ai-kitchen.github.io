Ralph Loops for Movie Recommendations

Featuring Cameron Behar, Co-founder & CTO of Sprinter Health.

Lucas 306 · 1:30 to 5:00 p.m. · Live tutorial at 2:30

What we'll cook

This Friday Cameron will show us how to use Claude as an agent to gather and synthesize real-world data (in this case, movie recommendations) and build a conversational bot you can actually interact with to decide what movies to watch. It is a hands-on introduction to agentic AI, data gathering, and lightweight market research using nothing but Claude.

Whether you are a solo builder, part of a small team, or just curious about what AI agents can actually do, this one is for you. No technical background needed.

About our guest

Our guest is Cameron Behar, Co-founder and CTO of Sprinter Health. With 15+ years of full-stack engineering experience across Google (YouTube, AdSense) and Meta (Oculus AR/VR), Cameron brings a rare mix of deep technical expertise and creative problem solving. Sprinter Health has raised a $55M Series B backed by a16z, Google Ventures, and General Catalyst, and now delivers at-home clinical care across 18 states.
