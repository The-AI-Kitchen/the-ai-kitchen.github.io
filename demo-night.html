June 5, 2026 · Demo Night

AI Kitchen Demo Night

Students from across SCU showcase the AI projects they built this quarter. Lightning pitches, gallery-style demos you can try for yourself, and a round of "best of" awards. Come see what students are cooking up.

5:30 to 7:30 p.m. · SCDI North Lobby · Free and open to the public

Run of show

Everything happens in SCDI, a few steps apart: the North Lobby (doors, demos, and awards) and room 1302 (opening remarks and the lightning pitches).

5:30
Doors open North Lobby
Check in, grab a bite, and settle in.
5:45
Welcome and lightning pitches SCDI 1302
We walk over to room 1302 for opening remarks (about 15 minutes), then each team gives a 90-second pitch. Pitches are timed to keep the lineup moving.
6:15
Demo showcase North Lobby
Back in the North Lobby, teams demo their projects at tables across the space. Circulate, try the products, and ask the builders anything.
7:00
Judging
Judges confer and pick the standouts while the demos keep running.
7:15
"Best of" awards North Lobby
Winners announced from the North Lobby stairwell.
7:30
Wrap
Keep mingling or head out, your call.

Demoing? Prepare two things

Every team does the same two things, so plan for both. The pitch earns attention; the table is where people actually try what you built.

Your two deliverables

  1. A 90-second pitch, live and timed, on stage in SCDI 1302 at 5:45. Rehearse to time. A clock runs and we keep the lineup moving, so practice landing it in 90 seconds.
  2. A table demo in the North Lobby from 6:15 to 7:00. Be ready to show your product to a circulating audience at your assigned table, back to back, for the full block.
Bring whatever you need to run your demo end to end: laptop and charger, any accounts pre-logged-in, and a backup plan (a short recording or screenshots) in case the wifi is slow. A one-line "what it is" and a "try this" prompt at your table go a long way.

Coming to watch

  • Free and open to the public. No RSVP needed, just show up.
  • Food and drinks in the North Lobby.
  • Come for the pitches at 5:45, or drop in for the hands-on demos at 6:15.
  • Bring a friend. The more people circulating the tables, the better the night is for the teams.
