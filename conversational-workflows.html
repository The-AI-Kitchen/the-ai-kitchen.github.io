AI Workflows for Real-World Conversations

Featuring Neo Zhang, MSx Candidate at Stanford GSB and former COO of Zhihu.

Lucas 306 · 1:30 to 5:00 p.m. · Live tutorial at 2:30

What we'll cook

Conversations are everywhere in real-world work: user interviews, support transcripts, sales calls, focus groups. They are also messy. This Friday Neo will walk us through a hands-on workflow for turning unstructured conversations into structured data you can analyze, search, and act on, using AI as the connective tissue.

Bring a laptop. Beginners are welcome. The exercise is designed to be approachable for any major.

About our guest

Neo Zhang is an MSx Candidate at the Stanford Graduate School of Business and formerly served as COO of Zhihu, one of the largest knowledge-sharing platforms in China. He brings deep operational experience in scaling AI-powered consumer products and is currently exploring how AI workflows can change the way teams turn conversations into insight.
